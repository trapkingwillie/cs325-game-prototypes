Police Revenge!

  • High Concept: The purpose of 'Police Revenge!' is to crash into the escaping criminal vehicle, and locate their deceased victims (stashed somewhere on the map), before time runs out.
  • Theme: This game utilizes the Storymatic theme of 'revenge'.
  • Mandated Variety: 'Police Revenge' utilizes direct keyboard directional key input, random locations of both the escaping cirminal vehicle and each victim (skeleton), along with a random tweening (escaping) speed and direction for the criminal vehicle, the 'Competitor' play style, and the 'racing' and 'puzzle' game genres.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players of 'Police Revenge!' should feel a sense of excitement and exhiliration after catching the criminals, along with intellectual stimulation when attempting to locate the victims of the criminals. Once both tasks are completed, players should feel a sense of revenge against the perpetrators of such terrible actions.
  • Gameplay: Players of 'Police Revenge!' seek to ram their police car (controlled through the directional keyboard keys) into the escaping criminal vehicle, the driver of which has recently disposed of three bodies around the playable area. To apprehend this criminal, players must click the 'arrest' button (the handcuffs). Once the perpetrator is apprehended and interrogated, he reveals the locations of his disposal sites - it is now the duty of the player to locate and collect the remains, in order to prosecute this criminal. In order to collect evidence, players must click the magnifying glass icon, after driving past all three vicitms. Once completed, the player has allowed OFC. Jones to get revenge on Mr. Doe by prosecuting him, and allowing closure for all victims and the city of Arlington.
  • Prototype Goal: 'Police Revenge' seeks to prototype the game mechanic of buttons, multiple collisions, sound effects triggered by buttons, and a story-driven means of creating core gameplay.
  • Features: 'Police Revenge' features random getaway speeds and directions for the criminal, random locations for all victims, and a story-driven core gameplay mechanic.
  • Setting/Premise: 'Police Revenge' is set in the city of Arlington, VA, and in the year 2250, where Mr. Doe has recently disposed of three of his latest murder victims. He was discovered at the site of a murder, revisiting the scene of a crime (just as the criminal profiler said he would). Police have surrounded him, and he has fled. It is now the duty of OFC. Jones to pursue, capture, and interrogate Mr. Jones about the location of his victims. OFC. Jones is tasked with getting revenge for the victims of Mr. Doe, and for the city of Arlington.
  • Story: The story for 'Police Revenge' follows serial murderer John Doe, who has recently committed multiple murders within the city of Arlington, Virginia. The Arlington County Police Department has spent countless months pursuing Mr. Doe, and has recently caught up to him at a prevoius murder scene. After detecting his tail, Mr. Doe has fled the scene in his flying car, and is now engaged in a pursuit with OFC. Jones, a two-year veteran of ACPD. It is now the duty of OFC. Jones to apprehend, interrogate, and recover the victims of Mr. Doe. OFC. Jones is tasked with getting revenge for the victims of Mr. Doe, and for the city of Arlington.
  • Target Audience: The target audience and demographic for 'Police Revenge' is anyone who enjoys action, puzzle, or story-driven games, adn those who enjoy futuristic story elements.
  • Play Time: 'Police Revenge' takes approximately one minute to play.
  • Strategies: Players with hand-eye coordination, spatial awareness, and attention to detail will do well in 'Police Revenge'.
